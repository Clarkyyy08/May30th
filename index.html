My Dearest Love

Forever & Always

May 30th fills my heart with a bittersweet joy. It's your birthday and our anniversary, two beautiful milestones we celebrate, even though miles separate us. Happy Birthday, my love. Though I can only see your face on a screen, the spark from our first meeting still burns bright. You light up my world, even from afar, and every virtual moment is a treasure. I hope your birthday is filled with happiness and the warmth of my love, carried across the distance. And then, there's our anniversary. Another year added to our long-distance story, a testament to patience, trust, and a love that knows no boundaries. We've navigated time zones, missed hugs, and whispered 'goodnights' into phones, and yet, our love has only deepened. I think back to our first date, the powerful connection we felt. Even now, with oceans or countries between us, that connection remains. We've built a love that transcends physical presence, a love that thrives on shared dreams and late-night calls. You are my confidante, my best friend, the one who understands me even through delayed conversations and exhaustion. You are the reason I smile, even through the ache of missing you. You are the reason I believe in forever, even when forever feels so far away.

The distance is a constant ache, and sometimes, I cry because I miss you so much. Today, as we celebrate your birthday and our anniversary, know that my love for you is unwavering. It bridges any gap, growing stronger with each passing day. You are my forever person, the one I choose, always. Given how much you mean to me, it breaks my heart that I'm so sorry for the things I've done that have brought you so much pain and trauma. I know saying sorry doesn't change anything, and I'm scared that our relationship almost ended because of me. Please don't leave me; I cannot imagine that you're not with me. I often get defeated by overthinking and by my actions that I wasn't even aware were causing you so much pain. I'm also deeply sorry for the pain I caused you during those six days of silence. I promise not to repeat that. I hope you just said it because you're hurt, you're tired, you're angry, and you overthink a lot. I hope that we can rebuild this relationship to be as strong as it was in the old days, and I hope you can accept me fully and trust me again. My deepest hope is that we can make this relationship last until our last breath. I may not be the perfect boyfriend, but I've found the perfect girlfriend and best friend in you. I appreciate you a lot. Thank you for staying, understanding, and loving me, even when it's hard, and even when we're apart. Thank you for being you, for creating a love that defies distance, for sharing your life with me.

I know it's hard for you to believe in me because of the trauma I've caused you, and I want you to know I'm not taking you for granted. And I know that you don't expect anything more from me to change, but I can prove you. That night, I was actually going to talk to you first, but I let you go with your friend so you could relax since you were stressed from work. So I just slept because I didn't want to overthink. I didn't expect you to act differently towards me, so I was shocked. About those 3 days you mentioned where you felt I was ignoring you, I wasn't ignoring you then. I feel like you got triggered because you thought I was suddenly going to Canada. That's why your approach towards me changed, and I was okay with that. I was just a bit cold because I was tired from arranging my visa. And speaking of a visa, it's not a visa for Canada; it's a visa for Korea. Why would I go to Canada when you said you would take me with you? I had already decided then that no matter what anyone here said to me, I would be with you because I want to fulfill your dream of living together. And I don't know if that will still happen because you said you don't know if you'll still take me. I've never ever lied to you except for white lies. I promise to cherish you, to support you, and to love you unconditionally, until we're finally together again. Always remember that I'm always here for you, no matter what.

Happy Birthday and Happy Anniversary, my love. Here's to closing the distance soon, and to celebrating many more beautiful years, in person.
